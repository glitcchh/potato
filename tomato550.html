Get to Know Seventeen
Seventeen is a K-Pop band that consists of 13 members, whom debuted on May 26, 2015, under Pledis Entertainment. Seventeen has been considered a "self-producing" idol group, with the members being actively involved in the songwriting and other aspects of the group.
